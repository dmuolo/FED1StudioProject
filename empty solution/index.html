About Us

We are a diverse team of guitar instructors representing a wide array of musical genres. We only have the best of the best, and we bring them right to you.

Just after the turn of the century, Ben Brown realized that music lessons shouldn't have to be all the way across town, so he started giving mobile music lessons. Once streaming technology began to improve, he took it one step further by bringing remote music lessons right to students across the world. Finally, he put together the "Avengers of Guitar" by recruiting the very best of the best across the globe to bring them right into your home.

Why Choose Us

We Have the Best Instructors

We have an elite team of instructors representing a wide array of genres. Our instructors are successful performing musicians that also possess an aptitude for teaching. Whether you're a blues or metal guitarist, we've got you covered.

We Bring Them Right to You
