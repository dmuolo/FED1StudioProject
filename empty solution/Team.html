Instructors

Muhammed Suicmez is a Turkish-German guitarist from the legendary band Necrophagist. From sweep picking to razor-sharp shredding, he's got you covered.

Ana Vidovic is a Croatian guitarist known for her richness in rendering many beloved classical guitar pieces. Learn the art of true skill and expression!
